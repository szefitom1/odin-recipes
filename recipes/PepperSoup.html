Stuffed Pepper Soup

Description

This stuffed pepper soup is easy to make for a hearty meal in one bowl. Great with warm bread for a comforting dinner.

Ingredients

  • Ground sirloin
  • Fresh veggies
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Chicken broth
  • Seasonings
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Cook the beef and drain the grease.

  2. Step 2

    Add the fresh veggies and cook until the onion is translucent.

  3. Step 3

    Add the tomatoes, broth, and seasonings. Simmer.

  4. Step 4

    Cook the rice, then stir the rice into the soup.
